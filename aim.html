APPLICATION INFRASTRUCTURE MANAGEMENT

Application Infrastructure Management

Application Infrastructure plays an important role in keeping disparate business applications, system components and services working cohesively.

Businesses need to ensure application availability, performance and increased uptime to safeguard as well as increase staff productivity, competitiveness and revenues. Changes to the ICT environment need to be carried out such that the desired outcomes are attained consistently preventing an adverse impact to business operations.

RETZ Technologies Application Infrastructure Management (AIM) services addresses these needs. Our AIM services manage the middleware layer, databases and packaged solutions, leveraging on our Centres of Excellence (CoEs) and technology partnerships with Product Principals.

Our AIM services interface with Application Management and Infrastructure Management services, to provide our customers a seamless service experience.

Our AIM service offering includes support operations, performance and availability management, administration, capacity management and optimization, release and patch management, upgrade and migration, backup and recovery, monitoring and reporting, for both on-premise and cloud environments.
