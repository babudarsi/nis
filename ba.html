Business Analytics

Business Analytics

Business Intelligence Solutions we offer:

1. Data Warehousing: We help convert your data into useful information using data warehousing software. Our data warehousing services include; data modelling, meta data management, data warehouse design, data transformation, data management and data quality management. We use advanced techniques and industry tools to create the perfect solutions for your business.

2. Data Visualisation: Our data visualisation services will help you quickly identify business patterns, trends and relationships between different elements of your business this includes both history and transactional data . Decision makers will be able to detect opportunities from structured and unstructured data through visually appealing reports, management dashboards and analytical reports.

3. Predictive Analysis: We gather data from multiple sources in your organisation's systems and provide valuable insights that improve revenues and customer loyalty at the same time. With our advanced analytical and statistical techniques, you will be able to predict business outcomes by analysing patterns and trends.

4. Marketing Automation: Our Marketing Automation solutions help your business build rewarding customer relationships by identifying your target audience and tracking the results of your campaign. We enable you to rapidly create and manage complex marketing campaigns using different channels as well as an intuitive visual interface that assists you from the planning stage to the execution stage.

5. Big Data: This service handles both structural and unstructural data from large data warehouses.  Our data collection, validation and integration techniques,  will reduce operational costs and maximise efficiency. We provide e-commercial and tailor made advanced solutions to suit your business needs.

6. Data Quality and Governance: The data quality framework we offervalidates the accuracy and timelines of the data as it flows from the source to the warehouse or business intelligence systems. Our data governance framework clearly defines the roles and responsibilities of each system, and establishes standards in data management compliance.

Data quality and governance services we offer include:

  • Providing scope for data governance planning and implementation
  • Implementing change management and other data quality projects
  • Ensuring that your business is following all data processing regulations
  • Ensuring that legacy contains updated data and follows current standards
