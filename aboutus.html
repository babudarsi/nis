Mission

Clients rely on NIS Import and Export to implement solutions that optimize their IT environments and maximize their return on technology investments. Whether your strategy is to go full public cloud, private cloud or a hybrid solution, you need a partner who understands corporate data center environments, virtualization, cloud migration and management of your mixed environment.

In addition, you want a partner with the experience to identify and mitigate risks associated with IT projects. NIS Import and Export is that partner and helping customers take full advantage of their technology investments is what we are all about.

Many Corporate IT projects get delayed or cancelled due to lack of resources or skill sets as IT departments continue to be asked to “do more with less.” NIS Import and Export to the rescue! Our professional services Consultants have depth and breadth of experience to help our clients implement their projects, clear their IT backlog and achieve business results. Whether implementing new solutions, optimizing current environments, performing technology refresh and upgrades, or providing day-to- day customized technical support, NIS Import and Export is your true business technology partner.

Our teams also help design alternate strategies and solutions to provide resolutions within the client's acceptable risk limit. We enable all enterprises striving to improve business effectiveness through the strategic use of technology and also help clients identify and capitalize on market opportunities in the technology marketplace.
